Who We Are

Built to navigate any market cycle

EMKAP commenced operations in December 2006 — and since then has steered through some of the most turbulent financial periods in modern history: the 2008 subprime crisis, the Eurozone debt crisis, Covid-19, and the geopolitical upheaval of recent years.

With a deep-rooted understanding of high-yield and emerging market bonds, our network spans established western institutions to frontier-market innovators. Our commitment to transparency and a client-focused approach ensures your strategy is positioned for growth.

Our KYC process is designed to be straightforward. Collect your identity and financial documents, submit your application with required KYC materials, sign agreements online, and await compliance approval — typically within 48 hours. Visit our "Open an Account" section above for the full step-by-step guide.
Funds may be transferred via bank wire or securities delivery. Please note that cash deposits are not accepted. Our team will provide you with full wire instructions upon account approval.
Clients have access to a secure, user-friendly client portal and our mobile trading application. Detailed trade confirmations and holdings reports are provided regularly and are available on request at any time.
Trade and settlement instructions can be submitted via our client portal or mobile application, as well as through various authorised communication channels as agreed during onboarding.
EMKAP Ltd (Cyprus) is regulated by CySEC. EMKAP UK Ltd is regulated by the FCA with direct Euroclear access. EMKAP Afrika Ltd is regulated by Nigeria's Securities and Exchange Commission. EMKAP US operates under applicable US regulations.
The EMKAP mobile app is available on the App Store and Google Play for most global users. Please note it is not available to U.S. investors. Download links are in the app section above.
